FAQ

Do you take walk-ins?

Although we have accepted walk-in appointments in the past, we currently do not take walk-ins, and you must schedule online or through the phone. You can schedule an appointment at our sign-up page!

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday through Friday, 8AM to 6PM, and Saturday from 8AM to 10PM!

Do you do birthday parties?

Yes, we do! And if the child is 12 years old or younger, he/she will receive a free small fry and milkshake from the McDonald's across the street.
